BMW

BMW, founded in 1916, is known for its luxury vehicles, motorcycles, and engines. The brand excels in performance and innovation.
Rolls Royce

Founded in 1906, Rolls Royce is synonymous with luxury, craftsmanship, and attention to detail, known for its high-end cars.
Land Rover

Land Rover, established in 1948, specializes in four-wheel-drive vehicles, renowned for durability and off-road capability.
Aston Martin

Founded in 1913, Aston Martin is famous for its luxury sports cars, combining elegance with high performance.
Ferrari

Ferrari, founded in 1939, is an iconic sports car manufacturer known for speed, racing heritage, and Italian craftsmanship.
Ford

Ford, established in 1903, revolutionized the automobile industry with mass production, notably through the Model T.
Volkswagen

Founded in 1937, Volkswagen is known for its reliable, affordable cars like the Beetle and Golf, and has a wide global reach.
Dodge

Founded in 1900, Dodge is an American brand known for its performance vehicles, including muscle cars and trucks.
Peugeot

Peugeot, established in 1810, is one of the oldest car manufacturers and is known for its French style and reliability.
TATA

Tata Motors, founded in 1945, is India's largest automotive manufacturer and has expanded its global presence significantly.
Bugatti

Founded in 1909, Bugatti is known for producing some of the most luxurious and fastest cars in the world, including the Veyron.
Porsche

Porsche, founded in 1931, is a German brand synonymous with high-performance sports cars, including the iconic 911.
Lamborghini

Founded in 1963, Lamborghini is known for its extravagant, high-performance sports cars with bold designs.
Mercedes

Mercedes-Benz, established in 1926, is a German luxury car manufacturer renowned for its innovation and engineering excellence.
